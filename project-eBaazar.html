eBazaar Online Ordering System

Java | JSP | Servlets | MySQL | Academic Project

eBazaar is an online bazaar ordering system that manages interactions between customers, vendors, and riders. Customers can browse products, place orders, and track deliveries. Vendors manage their product listings and orders. Riders handle delivery management efficiently. The system streamlines the order lifecycle, improving convenience and operational efficiency.

Features

  • Customer module: browse, search, order, and payment
  • Vendor module: manage products, track orders, update stock
  • Rider module: view assigned orders, update delivery status
  • Admin management for overseeing users and system operations
  • Session management with login/logout and role-based access
  • Order lifecycle management from placement to delivery
