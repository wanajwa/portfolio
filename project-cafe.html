Coffee and Dessert Cafe System

C++ | Academic Project

The Coffee and Dessert Cafe system is designed for admins (owners or staff) and customers. It automates menu and order management to reduce human errors, improve efficiency, and enhance customer satisfaction. Admins can manage menus and orders while customers can browse, add, and pay for items seamlessly.

Features

  • Menu management (insert, delete, update, sort)
  • Order management with queue system
  • Customer-friendly interface with search and cart
  • Online and physical payment support
  • Automatic receipt generation
  • Error handling for reliable operation
