Flora Bouquet Flower System

C++ | SQL Database | Academic Project

The Flora Bouquet Sales System replaces the manual system for staff handling stock and sales in bouquet flower shops. It automates stock tracking and sales management, reducing human errors and providing real-time stock updates.

Features

  • Automated bouquet stock tracking
  • Sales management and reporting
  • Inventory and category management for staff
  • Customer data management
  • Admin reporting for strategic insights
